RULES

1. The score will be increased by clicking on the bubble which is as same as the HIT indicator shows.

2. At every correct clicking on the bubbles, your score will be increased 10.

3. If you click in a bubble which not contains exact number the hit indicator shows then you will get a error message and also your score will be decreased 10.

4. You will get 60 seconds to play each round and at the end you will also get your final scores (if the score indicator not shows in your device while playing, then dont become freaked out. you will get your score at the end of the game).
